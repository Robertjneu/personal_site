overview

Causebox wanted help designing a homepage/product page for the launching their box subscription product. If you do not know Causebox, when you make a purchase they give a portion of the proceeds to a charity that you select before checking out. They wanted to focus more on the charity selection, than the actual box at first. It is important to note, this landing page was always meant to be a kick off for the product and changed as they began to gain more and more traction. The current site has a similar aesthetic based on the mood board.  

Objectives: Have initial homepage/product page and ordering flow done within a week. Differentiate themselves from other subscription boxes by giving to charity. Gain quick subscriptions to make the product an initial success.
